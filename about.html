About Mental Health Detection

Our Mental Health Detection system uses AI to analyze responses and provide insights into mental well-being. Our goal is to enhance mental health awareness and promote early detection.

Our Mission

We aim to make mental health support more **accessible and effective** through technology.

How It Works

  • Users answer scientifically designed mental health questions.
  • AI-powered analysis detects patterns and provides feedback.
  • Personalized mental health insights are displayed.
  • Users receive well-being suggestions and resources.
